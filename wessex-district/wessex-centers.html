Masonic Centers

The Chapters of the Wessex District meet at various Masonic centres throughout Hampshire and the Isle of Wight. Below are the principal meeting locations.

Bournemouth Masonic Hall

Address: Knole Road, Bournemouth, Dorset. BH1 4DH

Home to the Bournemouth Vigne and Sandeman Chapters.

Farnborough Masonic Centre

Address: Alexandra Road, Farnborough, Hants. GU14 6BS

A well-established centre serving the Faith Chapter and north Hampshire.

Bordon Masonic Hall

Address: High Street, Bordon, Hants. GU35 0AL

Home to the Army and Navy Chapter.

Winchester Masonic Centre

Address: Alresford Road, Winchester, Hants. SO21 1MB

Located near the ancient capital, home to the Winton Chapter.

Basingstoke Masonic Hall

Address: Victoria Street, Basingstoke, Hants. RG21 3BT

Home to the St. Michael Chapter.

Totton Masonic Hall

Address: Lexby Road, Totton, Southampton, Hants. SO40 9HD

Home to the New Forest and Tide Mill Chapters.

New Milton Masonic Hall

Address: Avenue Road, New Milton, Hants. BH25 5JP

Home to the St. Mary Magdalen Chapter.

Alton Masonic Temple

Address: Market Street, Alton, Hants. GU34 1HA

Home to the Gilbert White Chapter.

Kings Court Masonic Centre, Chandlers Ford

Address: Kings Court, Chandlers Ford, Eastleigh, Hants. SO53 2GG

Home to the Vigilate et Orate and Alfred the Great Chapters.

Andover Masonic Hall

Address: East Street, Andover, Hants. SP10 1EP

Home to the Pelican and Eagle Chapter.

Lymington Masonic Hall

Address: 10 High Street, Lymington, Hants. SO41 9AA

Home to the St. Thomas the Apostle Chapter.

Unity Hall, Ringwood

Address: Southampton Road, Ringwood, Hants. BH24 1HY

Home to the Unity St. John Chapter.

Visiting a Centre

If you plan to visit one of our Masonic centres, please contact the Chapter Recorder in advance to confirm meeting times and arrangements. Visitors should bring appropriate identification and proof of membership.
