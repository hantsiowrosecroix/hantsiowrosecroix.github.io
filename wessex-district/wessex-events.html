Meetings & Events

In addition to the regular Chapter meetings the Wessex District hosts various events during the year, such as Introduction to Rose Croix for master masons, a lights on run through of the Perfection ceremony and the Solent and Wessex District AGM.

Events

The District typically hosts the following events:

  • District Meeting: Annual business meeting for all members
  • Educational Lectures: Presentations on Rose Croix history, symbolism, and ceremonies

For more information about upcoming events or to suggest an event, please contact the District Recorder.
