Masonic Centers

The Chapters of the Solent District meet at various Masonic centres throughout Hampshire and the Isle of Wight. Below are the principal meeting locations.

Portsmouth Masonic Hall

Address: 110 High Street, Portsmouth, Hants. PO1 2HJ

Historic Masonic hall in Old Portsmouth, home to the Royal Naval Chapter.

Southampton Masonic Hall

Address: Freemasons Hall, Albion Place, Southampton, Hants. SO14 2DD

The main Masonic centre serving the Southampton area, home to the Canute and Fides et Justitia Chapters.

Ryde Masonic Hall, Isle of Wight

Address: John Street, Ryde, Isle of Wight. PO33 2PZ

A meeting place on the Isle of Wight, home to the Vectis Chapter.

Cosham Masonic Hall

Address: Albert Road, Cosham, Hants. PO6 3DD

Serving the Cosham area, home to the Portsdown and Portsmouth Chapters.

Gosport Masonic Hall

Address: Clarence Road, Gosport, Hants. PO12 1BB

Home to the Solent Chapter, serving the Gosport area.

Woolston Masonic Hall

Address: Manor Road, Woolston, Southampton, Hants. SO19 2DS

Home to the St. Olaf Chapter, serving the Woolston area of Southampton.

Fareham Masonic Hall

Address: Queens Road, Fareham, Hants. PO16 0NN

A meeting location for the Chapter of Sincerity and the Chapter of Revelation.

Horndean Masonic Hall

Address: 19a Five Heads Road, Horndean, Hants. PO8 9NW

Home to the Carnarvon and Durford Abbey Chapters.

Seaview Masonic Hall, Isle of Wight

Address: Fairy Road, Seaview, Isle of Wight. PO34 5HF

Home to the St. Boniface Chapter on the Isle of Wight.

Brook House Masonic Centre, Botley

Address: Brook Lane, Botley, Hants. SO30 2ER

Home to the Hamble River Chapter, serving the Botley area.

Visiting a Centre

If you plan to visit one of our Masonic centres, please contact the Chapter Recorder in advance to confirm meeting times and arrangements. Visitors should bring appropriate identification and proof of membership.
